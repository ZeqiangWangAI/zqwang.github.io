--- layout: page title: About Me permalink: / --- Welcome to my personal homepage. I am Zeqiang Wang. I am currently a PhD student in the Faculty of Engineering and Physical Sciences at the University of Surrey, funded by a full scholarship. Simultaneously, I serve as a Visiting Scholar at the Center for Applied Social and Economic Research (CASER) at NYU Shanghai. Previously, I obtained a Master of Research degree in Computer Science and Technology with Distinction from the University of Liverpool. My primary research direction focuses on the application of Natural Language Processing (NLP) in longitudinal social science and biomedical datasets, dedicated to solving complex interdisciplinary problems through artificial intelligence technologies. My specific research interests include measuring social biases in Large Language Models (LLMs), cross-lingual knowledge transfer of sociological concepts, and broader NLP-based computational social science research.

Email: zeqiang.wang@surrey.ac.uk  |  zeqiang.wang@nyu.edu
